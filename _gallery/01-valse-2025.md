## VALSE 2025 (视觉与学习青年学者研讨会)

**Date**: June 6-8, 2025  
**Location**: Zhuhai, China

I attended the Vision and Learning Youth Scholars Seminar (VALSE 2025) in Zhuhai, one of China's premier academic conferences focusing on computer vision and machine learning. This conference brought together young researchers and established scholars to discuss the latest advances in visual understanding, deep learning, and AI applications.

### Conference Highlights

- Attended keynote presentations from leading researchers in computer vision
- Participated in workshops on multimodal learning and generative models
- Networked with fellow students and researchers from top universities
- Learned about cutting-edge research in visual-language models
